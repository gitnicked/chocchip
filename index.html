Amazing Chocolate Chip Cookies

The only chocolate chip cookie recipe you'll ever need. Perfectly chewy, slightly crispy edges, and loaded with chocolate.

At a Glance

Prep Time

10 mins

Cook Time

12 mins

Chill Time

60 mins

Total Time

82 mins

Yield

~30 Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (227g) unsalted butter, very soft
  • 1 cup (213g) packed light brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup (100g) granulated sugar
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups (360g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 2 cups (12 oz) semi-sweet chocolate chunks/chips

Method

Beat butter, sugars, vanilla, and salt on medium-high speed until pale and fluffy (about 2 minutes). Use a stand mixer with a paddle attachment or a hand mixer. Scrape down the sides of the bowl as needed.
Add the eggs one at a time, beating for about 30 seconds after each addition until fully incorporated. Scrape down the sides and bottom of the bowl after adding each egg.
In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and baking powder. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and mix on low speed until just combined – be careful not to overmix. Gently stir in the chocolate chunks or chips using a spatula.
Cover the bowl tightly with plastic wrap and refrigerate the dough for at least 1 hour. For best results and deeper flavor, chill overnight (up to 3 days). Chilling is crucial for texture and preventing spreading.
When ready to bake, preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Line baking sheets with parchment paper or silicone baking mats.
Scoop rounded portions of the chilled dough (about 2 tablespoons each, a medium cookie scoop works well) onto the prepared baking sheets. Space them about 3 inches apart (usually 12 per standard sheet).
Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown and the centers look slightly underdone (they will continue to cook and set on the baking sheet). Rotate baking sheets halfway through if baking multiple sheets at once.
Let the cookies cool on the baking sheets for 5-10 minutes. This step is important for them to set properly. Then, carefully transfer the cookies to a wire rack to cool completely.
Once completely cooled, store the cookies in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 5 days. Enjoy your delicious homemade cookies!
